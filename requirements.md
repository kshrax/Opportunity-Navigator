1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose
Opportunity Navigator is an AI-powered web application designed to enable students to intelligently discover, evaluate, and track scholarships, internships, and programs tailored to their academic background, eligibility, and interests.
The platform transforms fragmented opportunity data into personalized, explainable, and actionable guidance.

1.2 Problem Overview
Students face significant challenges in accessing relevant opportunities due to fragmented information sources, complex eligibility criteria, and the absence of intelligent decision-support systems.
As a result, many students miss valuable opportunities despite eligibility.

2. Objectives

The system is designed to:
• Simplify opportunity discovery and navigation
• Deliver AI-driven personalized recommendations
• Interpret eligibility criteria through AI reasoning
• Provide explainable recommendation insights
• Prevent missed opportunities via deadline intelligence

3. Functional Requirements

3.1 User Profile Management
• Users shall be able to create and update profiles
• The system shall securely store academic and eligibility attributes
• Profile data shall be used for AI-driven personalization

3.2 Opportunity Matching
• The system shall generate AI-driven opportunity recommendations
• Opportunities shall be ranked using a Match Scoring mechanism
• Recommendations shall be personalized based on user profile data

3.3 Eligibility Reasoning
• The AI module shall interpret eligibility criteria
• The system shall provide explainable recommendation insights
• Eligibility confidence indicators shall be generated

3.4 Deadline Tracking
• The system shall track opportunity deadlines
• Smart alerts and reminders shall be generated
• Deadline risk indicators shall be supported

3.5 AI Assistant
• Users shall interact through natural language queries
• The system shall support conversational opportunity discovery
• AI responses shall provide contextual recommendations

4. Non-Functional Requirements

• The system shall support scalable cloud-native deployment
• AI interactions shall maintain low-latency response behavior
• User data shall be securely stored and accessed
• Architecture shall support cost-efficient serverless operation
• The system shall enable modular extensibility
